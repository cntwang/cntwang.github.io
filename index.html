Welcome! I'm Ruihang Chu (储瑞航), a 3rd year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia and Prof. Chi-Wing FU, Philip. Before that, I obtained the Master degree in State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, Beihang University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Yuru Zhang. I also have wonderful research experience with Prof. Xiaojuan Qi and Prof. Matthias Nießner.

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Machine Intelligence, particularly focusing on: 3D & Robotic Perception, Diffusion-based 3D Generation, and Image Understanding. My current research also includes Large Language Model.

Please refer to my Publication, Resume, and Google Scholar for more information.
